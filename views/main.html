Cali Come Mejor

Statistic's website

What would you find here?

This website contains statistical information for the project Cali Come Mejor. The results presented here were estimated by using the "2006 -2007 National Survey of Income and Expenses" (Encuesta Nacional de Ingresos y Gastos - ENIG).

For consulting the main results you can move to the Views' Section.There you can have access to tables, graphs and even to an interactive platform in which you can make your own queries to the used databases.
